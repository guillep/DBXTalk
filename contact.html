Contact

You can ask questions, report bugs, give feedbacks and anything you want using our mailing lists. We will also publish our work and releases. You can join here:

  • dbxtalk mailing list: questions regarding dbxtalk in general
  • glorp mailing list: questions regarding to glorp
  • pharo users mailing list: questions regarding the usage of Pharo

Nabble forums access

You can also access dbxtalk's mailing list from the Nabble forum. When using nabble forums, ensure that you are also subscribed to the mailing list. If not your posts will not be forwarded and we will not see them.
