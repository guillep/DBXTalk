Overview

I am an abstract paint, which should be converted before it can be used by Athens. I am backend agnostic, as opposed to concrete paint(s). Also, any other object can play the role of paint, as long as it implements the conversion method, #asAthensPaintOn:

See other implementors of #asAthensPaintOn:, such as Color and Form.
