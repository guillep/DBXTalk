Overview

A CalendarDyaMorph represents a specific day on a monthly calendar.

Instance Variables bounds: date: highlighted: owner:

bounds - owner-relative bounding box

date - the specific date (year/month/day) the CalendarMorph represents

highlighted - flag to keep track of when a CalendarMorph has the mouse dragging over it, and is thus highlighted

owner - the morph that contains the CalendarMorph, and all its siblings
